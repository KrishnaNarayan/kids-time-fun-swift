Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Island Innovation LLC built the Kids Time Fun app. This page explains how the app handles information. The short version: Kids Time Fun does not collect, store, or share any personal information whatsoever.

Information we collect

None. The app does not ask for, collect, or transmit any personal data. There are no accounts, no sign-in, and no forms.

Data stored on your device

Game progress and high scores are saved only on your own device so you can pick up where you left off. This information never leaves the device and is never sent to us or to anyone else. Deleting the app removes it.

Tracking and analytics

The app does not track you. It contains no third-party analytics, no advertising, no advertising identifiers, and no tracking technologies of any kind. The app does not connect to the internet to send your data.

Children's privacy

Kids Time Fun is designed for children and families. Because the app collects no personal information at all, it does not knowingly collect anything from children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country). The app contains no ads, no in-app purchases, and no links that lead children out of the app without a parent's involvement. Our practices are intended to align with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Apple's guidelines for apps made for kids.

Third parties

The app does not share any information with third parties, because it does not collect any.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, we will update this page and revise the date below. Continued use of the app after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.
